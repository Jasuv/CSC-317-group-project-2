Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping Questions

  • How long does shipping take?

    Since all of our services are delivered digitally via email, there’s no physical shipping involved. Most services are completed within 3-5 business days.

  • Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes! Our services are available to clients worldwide as everything is delivered via email.

  • What are the shipping costs?

    There are no shipping costs since all our services are delivered electronically.

Returns Policy

  • What is your return policy?

    We offer revisions on completed projects to ensure satisfaction. However, as digital services are customized, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

  • How do I initiate a return?

    If you’re unsatisfied, please contact our support team at support@codequad.com within 7 days of delivery, and we’ll work to address your concerns.

  • Are there items that cannot be returned?

    Due to the nature of digital services, once the final files have been delivered, we cannot accept returns unless there’s a clear issue with the service.

Account Questions

  • How do I create an account?

    You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and filling out the required details.

  • What do I do if I forget my password?

    Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, and we’ll email you instructions to reset your password.

  • How can I update my account information?

    Log into your account, navigate to the "Account Settings" section, and update your details as needed.

Payment Questions

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods.

  • Is my payment information secure?

    Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your payment information.

  • Can I use multiple payment methods?

    Currently, we only accept one payment method per transaction.

General Info

  • Where is your company located?

    We are a fully remote team, and our operations are conducted entirely online.

  • How do I contact customer support?

    You can reach our support team at support@codequad.com. We’re here to help!

  • Do you have physical store locations?

    No, we operate exclusively online to provide our services efficiently and globally.
